Programming, Justice and Life lessons

Refilwe Modise
May 27

Having constant communication with social groups in the midst's of the design process is very helpful in forming more ethical and just ideas on how something should be used/portrayed, whether it be app, websites and games, broadening your perspective on how we design. What I think could also be taken into consideration is what I am and I am programming. From important information to take notes of for other coworkers to understanding what the information I will be gathering would be sued for, so that I can still protect the privacy and security of an consumer.
