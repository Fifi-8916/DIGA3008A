Progress...Report...I don't know...

by Refilwe Modise March 3

I’ve been able to make a lot of progress in terms of core programming, I now have 3 pages, home, blog and gallery. I have also created a navigation bar on the home page and now I’m able to move to the other two pages, not back yet, but since the initial navigation bar is working fine, I’m more confident that other will work well. This week I’m planning to finish the navigation bar for all pages, add extra pages containing personal information and project showcases and add all the missing information that I’m going to be written. I’m hoping that it will create a create and artistic idea of what I have in mind, but I’ll have to first get through the basic stuff before I get to this.
