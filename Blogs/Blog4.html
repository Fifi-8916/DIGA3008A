Progress Report Part 2

by Refilwe Modise March 10

I have made a lot of progress this week. Started making use of micro formatting for some minor elements such sections and article for organising, hr lines to help make sure my sentences are in the writes lines and an early aside function for future use in when styling in CSS. Speaking of CSS, if I was understanding correctly, div and span are used to as containers that help with styling for CSS, so it makes sense why we would be hammered to not use div and span. What is also interesting is the use of attributes to either reference, classify and identify certain portions of the script, praying this makes it easier in CSS and JavaScript; it has helped me in small features that make my website more interactable.

Overall, my website, though very repetitive provides me with the basic outline, I’m able to move to and from different pages and I hope this week to add all my basic information for the website. In terms of design, I’ve made designs for all my pages with basic wireframing, this helped me order my website design formatting, I would’ve been stuck if I didn’t do that. Praying that God helps me in this endeavour and that nothing bad will happen…hopefully.
