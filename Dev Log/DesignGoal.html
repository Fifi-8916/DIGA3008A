Design Goal

by Refilwe Modise

I want you to enter my dream world, the vastness of my creativity and ingenuity brought to light. I am often a day dreamer so I think it would be better to show off what I think than to just explain it all to you. I hope you were able to find my puzzle. It shouldn't be hard just call me when you have an answer. By the way, this isn't me being crazy, I just want to speak my thoughts the best way I can. So I hope you're enjoying the experience. :)

The night sky with me! I hope you...en...joy
